Research Interests

I work on artificial intelligent and networking. My major research interests are Natural Language Processing and Computer Networks, especially the intersection of them, such as Distributed NLP, Network Configuration, and Network Modeling.

I work at NIRC (Wechat:BUPT_NIRC).Welcome to visit us!

I’m looking for highly self-motivated students to work with me as PhD and/or master students. Candidates who are interested please contact me directly.
